    Translation by Emmanuel Francis

    1Prosperity!

    1[Date/Day] 25, of the month of Kārttikai, [bright fortnight], ... when the glorious ... the glorious heroic Lord Viruppaṇa↓1 was currently ruling the earth.

    1-3So that there be (āka) ... a land of 240 mās, [as follows]:

    • a land of 40 mās ...,
    • a land of 80 mās ...,
    • [a land of 40 mās] ...,
    • a land of 20 mās ...,
    • a land of 20 mās ...,
    • a land of 20 mās ...,
    • a land of 20 mās ...,
    [there is] a land of 240 mās.

    3This land of two hundred fourty (240) mās, ... for the donation ... [...]↓2
